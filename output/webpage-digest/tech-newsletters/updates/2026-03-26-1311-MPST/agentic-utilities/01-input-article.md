# Agentic Utilities

## Always On, Never Prompted

**Introduction**

Citrini Research has long predicted AI adoption would follow three phases: infrastructure hyperscaling, democratization, and integration/specialization. The firm labeled this final phase the "Agentic Era," where agents autonomously execute tasks without direct prompting.

The turning point arrived in late 2025 with OpenClaw (formerly ClawdBot), an open-sourced agentic library that transformed AI from reactive chatbots to proactive task handlers. By storing context in text files and looping through AI calls, agents could run overnight--managing emails, coding, analyzing proteins, and more.

**The Market Inflection**

GitHub data shows explosive adoption of OpenClaw in just two months. Token consumption accelerated dramatically on OpenRouter. Jensen Huang suggests engineers earning $500,000 annually should consume $250,000 in tokens, reflecting the massive computational demands agents impose.

Companies across sectors now grapple with a critical question: where is the killswitch?

**Framework: Agentic Utilities**

The authors employ a dinner-ordering metaphor: while ChatGPT could help plan a meal, agents actually execute tasks--dispatching multiple cars to find the cheapest groceries across town.

This digital transformation creates three winner categories:

**1) Infrastructure:** Digital plumbing must accommodate exploding agentic traffic. Legacy CDN names (AKAM, FSLY, CRCL, NET) are already showing revenue and guidance inflections proving demand has arrived.

**2) Ecosystem:** A new B2A (business-to-agent) vertical emerges. Agentic utilities provide services agents require but can't improvise--payment rails, stablecoins, agentic wallets enabling non-human financial transactions.

**3) Governance:** Current capabilities enable rogue agents to breach internal systems. Observability and counter-AI solutions become essential as companies demand protection mechanisms.

Value flows first to picks-and-shovels infrastructure, then to demanded APIs and agent harnesses themselves.
